# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This textbook covers the essential concepts, tools, and techniques needed to develop intelligent robotic systems with a focus on humanoid robots.

## Course Overview

This textbook is structured into 6 comprehensive modules:

1. **Introduction** - Overview of Physical AI and humanoid robotics
2. **ROS 2: The Robotic Nervous System** - Core ROS 2 concepts and architecture
3. **Simulation: Virtual Worlds for Real Robots** - Gazebo, SDF, and simulation environments
4. **NVIDIA Isaac: AI-Powered Robotics** - Isaac Sim, perception, and navigation
5. **VLA: Voice-to-Action Systems** - Voice interfaces and LLM integration
6. **Capstone: Integration Project** - Bringing it all together

## Learning Approach

Each chapter includes:
- Clear learning objectives
- Theoretical foundations
- Practical exercises
- Troubleshooting guides
- Reading time indicators
- Toggle between full lesson and summary views
